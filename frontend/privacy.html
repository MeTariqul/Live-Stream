Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your username and a hashed password. We do not collect your email address, real name, or payment information. We may collect anonymous usage data such as which channels you watch, for the purpose of watch history and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the live TV streaming service
  • To remember your preferences (favorites, last-watched channel)
  • To display anonymous analytics to the platform administrator
  • To send program reminders and notifications (with your permission)

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored in Vercel KV (a Redis-compatible key-value store). Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with cost factor 12. Video streams are processed through Mux; Mux's privacy policy applies to video content. Uploaded files (channel icons, logos, subtitles) are stored in Vercel Blob.

4. Cookies

We use a single httpOnly cookie to store your JWT authentication token. This cookie is essential for the service to function. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies are used.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Video streams are processed by Mux; recordings may be stored in Vercel Blob.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your profile information at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from your profile page. This removes your user record, favorites, watch history, and reminders from KV.
  • Correction: You can change your password from your profile page.

7. Account Deletion

To delete your account, go to your Profile page and click "Delete Account". You will be asked to confirm your password. All your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the platform administrator.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
